FLUIDITY in simulated human-robot interaction with speech interfaces

About

This is the website for the FLUIDITY in simulated human-robot interaction with speech interfaces (EPSRC 2023-2026) project.

This is a three-year project hosted by Swansea University and partnered with the University of Hertfordshire.
